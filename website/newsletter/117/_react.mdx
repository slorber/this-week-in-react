**[Next.js Layouts RFC in 5 minutes](https://vercel.com/blog/next-js-layouts-rfc-in-5-minutes)**

The file-system based Next.js router will be significantly upgraded in the next major release. It includes support for nested layouts (like in Remix). This allows to avoid unnecessary unmount/remount of a layout during browsing, but also to manage data loading and errors. This article gives a quick overview. See also [Delba's thread](https://twitter.com/delba_oliveira/status/1568291558776274945).
